gun

In this project, I prepared a comprehensive ecosystem called GUN, facilitating the development of community-run and encrypted applications. Analogous to an Open Source Firebase or a Decentralized Dropbox, GUN is utilized by prominent entities like the Internet Archive and was integrated into Twitter's bluesky initiative. It offers multiplayer functionality with real-time peer-to-peer state synchronization, supports diverse data structures including key/value pairs, tables, documents, and videos, and ensures local-first, offline, and decentralized operations with end-to-end encryption.
